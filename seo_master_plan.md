# AcuTherapy Clinics - Ultimate Local Medical SEO Master Plan

This document summarizes the comprehensive SEO, GEO, and content strategy for **AcuTherapy Clinics**, aiming to achieve Top 3 Google Local Rankings in Honolulu.

## 1. Website Repositioning: From "Wellness" to "Medical Clinic"
*   **Target Audience:** Pain management, injury recovery, auto accident, workers' comp.
*   **Core Message:** Proven, medical-grade acupuncture backed by 30+ years of clinical experience.
*   **Keyword Strategy:** Transitioning from generic "acupuncture honolulu" to high-intent symptom and situation keywords (e.g., "auto accident acupuncture honolulu", "whiplash treatment honolulu").

## 2. Page Structure & Topic Clusters (53 Pages)
The website adopts a strong "Topic Cluster" architecture:

### 🔥 Core Pages & Service Pages
*   **Homepage** (`/`) - The authoritative hub showcasing Dr. Cai's E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authority, Trust).
*   **Services** - `Acupuncture Honolulu`, `Medical Massage`, `Fire Cupping Therapy`.
*   **High-Value Injury Pages** - `Auto Accident Injury`, `Workers Comp Acupuncture`.

### ⚡ Symptom Pages (Money Pages)
*   Back Pain, Sciatica, Neck Pain, Shoulder Pain, Knee Pain, Migraines, Whiplash.
*   **Internal Linking:** Symptom pages interlink and push authority up to core service pages.

### 📍 GEO Location Pages
*   Waikiki, Kahala, Manoa, Hawaii Kai, Kaimuki, Ala Moana, Kapahulu, Moiliili.
*   *Strategy:* Capture "acupuncture near [neighborhood]" searches without cluttering the main navigation.

### 📝 Blog & Long-Tail Pages
*   Answering specific patient queries (e.g., "Does acupuncture help sciatica?", "How many sessions for whiplash?").

### 🌏 Multi-Language Landing Pages
*   Chinese, Japanese, and Korean landing pages specifically targeting Asian tourists and local multi-lingual residents for high-end or culturally-aligned treatments.

## 3. On-Page SEO Implementation Checklist
*   ✅ **URL Structure:** Clean, exact-match URLs (e.g., `/sciatica-acupuncture-honolulu`).
*   ✅ **Title Tag & Meta Description:** Optimized with primary keywords and click-inducing CTA.
*   ✅ **Structured Data (JSON-LD):** `MedicalBusiness`, `MedicalWebPage`, `Physician`, and `FAQPage` schemas injected to inform Google of exact local coordinates and medical specialties.
*   ✅ **H1/H2 Hierarchy:** Proper semantic breakdown of content (e.g., H1: Main keyword, H2: Causes, Treatments, Insurance).
*   ✅ **Image ALT Tags:** Descriptive and keyword-rich (e.g., `sciatica-nerve-pain-acupuncture-diagram`).
*   ✅ **Internal Linking Web:** Specific anchor texts (e.g., "sciatica treatment" instead of "click here") routing users between Blog -> Symptom -> Service.
*   ✅ **TCM Integration:** Content explains *how* acupuncture works medically (blood flow, endorphins, inflammation) and traditionally (TCM meridians).

## 4. Google Business Profile (GBP) Synergy
*   Weekly posts focused on specific symptoms and GEO locations to signal active, local, and relevant business operation to Google Maps.
