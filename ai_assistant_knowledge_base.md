## 5. First Visit & Treatment Experience (首次就诊与治疗体验)

**English FAQ:**
**Q: What should I expect on my first visit? Does acupuncture hurt?**
A: For your first visit, please arrive 10-15 minutes early to complete any health intake forms. Dr. Cai will perform a thorough assessment of your condition. The initial consultation and treatment usually take about 45-60 minutes. Follow-up treatments take roughly 30-45 minutes.
**Acupuncture does not usually hurt.** We use sterile, single-use, hair-thin needles. You might feel a slight tap initially, followed by a dull ache, tingling, or heavy sensation (this is called "De Qi" and indicates the energy blockages are clearing). The experience is highly relaxing, and many patients fall asleep during treatment.

**中文问答:**
**Q: 第一次来需要准备什么？针灸过程会痛吗？**
A: 首次就诊建议提前 10-15 分钟到达填写基础健康评估表。蔡医生会为您进行详尽的问诊与体查。首次涵盖咨询与完整治疗的流程通常需要 45-60 分钟。后续复诊约 30-45 分钟。
**针灸通常是不会痛的。**我们使用的是经过严格无菌处理的极细一次性医疗针。绝大多数患者只会感到像蚊子叮咬般的极轻微触碰，随后会在穴位深处产生酸胀、沉重或酥麻的“得气”感，这是能量和血液正在疏通的好现象。整个治疗过程非常舒缓，许多患者甚至会在治疗床上放松入睡。

## 6. Emergencies & Policy (紧急情况处理)

**English FAQ:**
**Q: Do you offer emergency care or walk-in urgent care?**
A: We are a specialized physical rehabilitation and pain management clinic, so we do **NOT** provide 24/7 emergency services. If you are experiencing chest pain, difficulty breathing, strokes, or sudden severe trauma, please **dial 911** or go to the nearest hospital Emergency Room immediately.

**中文问答:**
**Q: 你们晚上或者周末可以接急诊吗？**
A: 本诊所为慢病康复与疼痛专科诊所，**不提供 24 小时急诊服务**。如遇极为严重的胸部挤压痛、呼吸困难、中风征兆或突发剧烈创伤倒地，请立刻 **拨打 911** 报警求助，或立刻前往最近的医院急诊室 (ER)。
